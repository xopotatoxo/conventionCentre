COMING FIRST TIME?

What do I bring?

  • Water Bottle
  • Snacks
  • Reusable Shopping Bag
  • Comfortable Shoes
  • Portable Charger

What to Expect?

  • Independent vendors and artists selling merch for all your favourite properties as well as unique art pieces, trinkets, and everything else under the sun
  • Panels with your favourite celebrities
  • Workshops on topics such as cosplay construction, stage combat, and more
  • Opportunities for autographs and photo ops with celebrities
  • Food trucks outside and food vendors in addition to the food court inside

FAQ

  • Are there ATMs on site?
    • Yes, ATMs are available.
  • How much is it to park on site?
    • The cost to park is $25 per day.
  • Where is the nearest C-train station?
    • Around 300m away - Victoria Park/Stampede Station.
  • Questions on the day? Ask one of our volunteers in the blue EXPO shirts!
