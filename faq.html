FAQ

What should I bring to the convention?

We recommend bringing your ID, convention ticket or pass, a small backpack, snacks, water, portable charger, and any personal necessities you might need.

What should I expect?

  • Independent vendors and artists selling merch for all your favourite properties as well as unique art pieces, trinkets, and everything else under the sun
  • Panels with your favourite celebrities
  • Workshops on topics such as cosplay construction, stage combat, and more
  • Opportunities for autographs and photo ops with celebrities
  • Food trucks outside and food vendors in addition to the food court inside
  • Other wonderful surprises!

Are there ATMs at the convention?

Yes, there are several ATMs located throughout the convention center for your convenience.

Is there a dress code?

There's no formal dress code, but we encourage attendees to wear comfortable attire. Cosplaying is welcome and encouraged!

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Yes, you can bring your own snacks and beverages, but keep in mind there are also a variety of food vendors available at the event.

How much does parking cost?

On-site parking costs $25 per day.

Where is the nearest CTrain station?

Victoria Park/Stampede Station.

Any additional questions?

Contact us at 719-266-2837 or ask any of our volunteers in the blue EXPO shirts!
