Vendor Directory User Guide

Welcome to the EventEASE Vendor Directory! This page allows you to browse and interact with a list of vendors participating in the event.

  • Searching for Vendors: Use the search bar at the top of the page to find vendors by name. As you type, the list will update to show only those vendors matching your search query. You can also press the star to filter by favourites.
  • Viewing Vendor Details: Click on any vendor to view more information about them. You will be taken to a dedicated page with details such as their products, location, and contact info.
  • Bookmarking Vendors: You can bookmark vendors for quick access later. Just click the 'Bookmark' button next to a vendor's name. Bookmarked vendors can be viewed by toggling the filter in the search bar.

The navbar at the bottom of the screen helps you navigate through the EventEASE app. Use the home icon to return to the main menu, and the question mark icon to open this help dialog.

If you need further assistance or have any questions, please reach out to our support team at support@EventEase.com or call us at 719-266-2837.
